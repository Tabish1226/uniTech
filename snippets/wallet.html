Capital Refund Procedure

Your capital with UTN is safe and secure and you can withdraw it any time according to the following rule.

  • Within 3 to 6 months of package activation 40% deduction.
  • Within 6 to 12 months of package activation 25% deduction.
  • After 12 months of package activation 0% deduction.

No service charges will be applied on capital withdraw.

Deposits and Withdrawals

You can make Deposits & Withdrawals through Internal transfers, Perfect Money, USDT and Bank wire.

  • Service charges for internal transfer 2% (within a second)
  • Service charges for Perfect Money 6% (24 hours)
  • Service charges for USDT 8% (48 hours)
  • Service charges for bank transfer 10% (5 working days)

On 30th of every month (including weekends) your monthly ROI will be transferred to your cashbox and on 1st of every month you will be able to cash out your amount through your desired international wallet or local banks. Internal transfers can be done anytime 24/7 around the clock.
